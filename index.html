Here's a Timeline of Sushant Singh Rajput's Life

  • 1986 - Born on 21 January, in Patna, Bihar, India.
  • 2003 - He was extremely brilliant at studies as he secured all-India 7th rank in AIEEE. However, he was inclined towards dancing therefore he dropped out of engineering and devoted himself full-time to dancing and acting.
  • 2006 - He featured as a background dancer in the Dhoom Again song of Dhoom 2 and in Ashwariya Rai's performance at the closing ceremony of the 2006 Commonwealth Games. Also he dropped out of his engineering course during his fourth year to begin a career in arts and performance.
  • 2008 - He was subsequently called for an audition and cast as a second lead in the television series Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil in 2008.
  • 2009 - Later he was casted as the lead in another series Pavitra Rishta. The role was reportedly a breakthrough for him and for which he won various television awards.
  • 2010 - Won his first award for best actor in the Indian Television Academy Awards 2010.
  • 2013 - Made his first debut into Bollywood with a film named Kai Po Che.
  • 2013-2020 - Acted in 12 films namely Kai Po Che!(2013), Shudh Desi Romance(2013), PK(2014), Detective Byomkesh Bakshi(2015), M.S Dhoni - The untold story(2016), Raabta(2017), Wecome to NewYork(Cameo Appearance)(2018), Kedarnath(2018), Sonchiriya(2019), Chhichhore(2019), Drive(2019), Dil Bechara(2020)
